FAQ

  • How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

    To print, you must go to http://print.hz.nl and send the file you wish to print to the printer in A3 or A4 format. Next you must go to the printer and register with your school ID there. After doing so you can see and print your file for up to 24 hours after requesting to print.

  • How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

    If you wish to scan a document you must go to a printer and log in with your school ID. Before logging in you need to be sure you have at least 7 cents on your HZ ID. When you have logged in you need to select the option scan and place your document on the glass part of the printer. Now you need to press 'scan and send', 'send to me', 'yes' and then the 'start' button. After this there will be a pop up on the printer on which you must press 'start sending'. When you have completed scanning you need to press 'stop' and 'log out'. Now you should have succesfully scanned your document.

  • How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

    If you want to book a project space you need to go to:
    self-service portal > new reservation > book project group rooms
    Here you can choose a space you want to book. After choosing a place you need to fill out some details like what you are doing and with whom. That's it, after that you have succesfully booked a project space.

  • What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

    You can park at Poelendaelesingel 10 by using your HZ ID or by making a request in the self-service portal and talking in the intercom by the guard-arm. There are some charging stations for electric cars at the parking space and there are also two parking spaces for the disabled right in front of the HZ building.
